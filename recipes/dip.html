Buffalo Dip

Description

A savory and spicy dip version of the classic chicken wing! Can be held in a crockpot after cooking to last the whole party long!

Ingredients

  • Two packs cream cheese
  • 1 can of canned chicken
  • 3/4 cup buffalo sauce
  • 1/2 cup chunky blue cheese dressing
  • 1/2 cup ranch dressing
  • shredded mexican or cheddar cheese

Steps

  1. Soften two packs of cream cheese in microwave about one minute.
  2. In a large fry pan use wooden spoon to break up chicken pieces as little as possible.
  3. Add softened cream cheese and stir as smooth as you can.
  4. Stir in the buffalo sauce, followed by the ranch and blue cheese.
  5. Turn on heat to medium-low and stir in a handful of the shredded cheese.
  6. Heat until mixture is bubbling on the edges and place in a crockpot on low.
  7. Layer the top with more shredded cheese and keep on low until heated throughout.
  8. Serve with tortillas, crackers, or celery sticks!
