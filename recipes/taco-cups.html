Crunchy Taco Cups

Description

Crispy and delicious taco flavor in a grab and go cup! Using wonton wraps in muffin pans this savory party or game day food will certainly be a fan favorite.

Ingredients

  • Pack of wonton wraps (requires 48 wraps)
  • Twp pounds hamburger
  • One can diced tomatoes and green chilis (Rotel)
  • Shredded Mexican cheese
  • Taco seasoning
  • Your favorite taco toppings!

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees.
  2. Brown the hamburg and spray each cup of two muffin pans with non-stick cooking spray.
  3. Drain the hamburg really well as as little grease as possible is desired. Add to large mixing bowl.
  4. Drain the tomatoes and add to bowl with desired amount of taco seasoning and stir well.
  5. Place a wonton wrap into each cup and place a spoonful of the meat mixture in each one.
  6. Add cheese to each cup and then add a second layer of wraps/meat mixture/cheese.
  7. Bake for around ten minutes or until edges of cups are browned and crispy.
  8. Remove from muffin pans and serve with sour cream, salsa, and taco sauce or any other of you favorite taco toppings.
