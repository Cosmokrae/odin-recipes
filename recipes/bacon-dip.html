Bacon Dip

Description

A creamy and cheesy dip full of bacon flavor! Another party crockpot favorite!

Ingredients

  • 2 packs cream cheese
  • 16 ounce tub of light sour cream
  • Mayo
  • Bacon bits or pieces
  • Shredded mexican or cheddar cheese

Steps

  1. Soften the cream cheese in microwave about one minute and add to large mixing bowl.
  2. Add tub of sour cream.
  3. Clean out sour ceam tub and fill fairly full with mayo and add to bowl.
  4. Mix thouroughly.
  5. Add a large handful of shredded cheese and mix in.
  6. Stir in a handful of bacon.
  7. Heat mixing bowl in microwave, maybe two miutes, stir, and place contents in a small crockpot.
  8. Turn on low and mix occasionally until heated throughout and then garnish top with bacon pieces.
  9. Serve with tortillas or crackers.
